Material Handling Equipment

Precision-engineered components designed to regulate, meter, and discharge bulk solids efficiently.

Rotary Airlock Valve

Precision-engineered valves designed to regulate the flow of bulk materials between different process areas while maintaining airtight seals, preventing air leakage, and maintaining pressure differentials.

Benefits

  • Airtight sealing
  • Continuous material flow
  • Reliable dust collection performance

Screw Feeder

Metering and feeding devices utilizing rotating screws to deliver controlled amounts of bulk materials to downstream processes. Features adjustable feed rates to accommodate varying material properties.

Benefits

  • Accurate dosing & uniform flow
  • Ideal for powders and pellets
  • Supports food & pharma processing

Vibrating Tubular Feeder

Vibratory feeders designed for extremely gentle handling and precise metering. Features enclosed tubular designs for dust-free operation and optional discharge chutes for controlled material flow.

Benefits

  • Dust-free gentle handling
  • Minimal maintenance required
  • Perfect for fragile ceramics/plastics

Silo Discharge Equipment

Specialized components ensuring reliable material discharge from storage silos. Includes fluidizing pads, vibrators, and discharge gates engineered to prevent material bridging and ensure consistent flow.

Benefits

  • Prevents severe material blockages
  • Reduces downtime
  • Improves silo extraction efficiency
