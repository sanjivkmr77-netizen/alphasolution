About Us

Our Foundation

Alpha Solutions was established in 2018 as a Proprietorship firm under the visionary leadership of Mrs. Neelam Singh, with a focused specialization in Hydraulic Hose Pipes and Precision Assemblies. What began as a dedicated hydraulic solutions enterprise has strategically evolved into a diversified, multi-disciplinary engineering organization serving leading industrial clients across India.

In its foundational phase, Alpha Solutions concentrated exclusively on delivering high-performance hydraulic hose assemblies and fittings, engineered for reliability, safety, and long operational life.

"Through uncompromising quality standards, precision assembly practices, and strong technical expertise, the company quickly earned the confidence of clients in sectors such as construction, mining, heavy engineering, automotive, and agriculture."

2020 Expansion & Beyond

Building on this strong foundation, the company expanded its portfolio in 2020 to address broader industrial requirements. Today, Alpha Solutions offers comprehensive solutions across:

  • Plant & Product Engineering Services
  • Air Pollution Control & Gas Cleaning Systems
  • Bulk Material Handling Systems
  • Electrical & Industrial Automation Solutions
  • EV Spare Parts & Components
  • Energy-Efficient LED Lighting Solutions
